Project Overview

Eliminating productivity bottlenecks in post-close operations

üéØ Business Problem

üí° Solution

BoB Management Suite decouples bundling from Byte LOS, enabling background processing. Users remain productive, process multiple loans asynchronously, and can log out during operations. Built using our proven CLEAR portal approach.
