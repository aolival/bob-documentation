Project Overview

Eliminating productivity bottlenecks in post-close operations

üéØ Business Problem

Current bundle operations lock users out of Byte LOS for 5-90+ minutes during document processing, creating critical productivity bottlenecks in post-close shipping operations.

üí° Solution

BoB Management Suite: Standalone web application that decouples document bundling from Byte LOS, enabling background processing while users remain productive. Users can process multiple loans asynchronously and log out during long-running operations.

üìä Expected Impact

Target Users: Shippers (Business Units, Employee Loans, Retail Corporate), Trade Group, Post-Closing Review Group
Productivity Gain: Eliminates 5-90 minute system lockouts per bundle operation
Operational Efficiency: Background job processing with 6 parallel work streams
